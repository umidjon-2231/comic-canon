# S01-006 — Heard

## Beat
Sael descends with his listeners, calm as ever, and orders the room — and Vela — still. But the
deaf can't be commanded (Law 2), and Kai is already moving. She doesn't fight him; she presses the
coin to the archive's old broadcast horn and commands it to *speak* — flinging his recorded order
across every tier that can hear. The undercity hears the Sealing was murder. Sael, for the first
time, looks afraid of his own voice, and withdraws. Kai has won the tier and lost her anonymity.
