# S01-004 — A Measured Length

## Beat
Vela teaches Kai the second half of Resonance: not just hearing what an object remembers, but
telling it what to do — once. Kai over-spends on her first real command and a streak of her hair
goes white. Vela presses the brass fork into her hands: the only way she'll survive opening the
archive is to dampen the cost.
