# S01-002 — The Sealed Tier

## Beat
Running from the listeners, Kai tries to drop the coin in the deep water — but it won't go quiet,
and its hum pulls her down to a brass door sealed shut since the Sealing. She can feel a command
holding it closed. She has no idea how to break one.
