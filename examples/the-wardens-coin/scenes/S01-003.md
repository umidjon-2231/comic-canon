# S01-003 — The Tuner

## Beat
Cornered, Kai ducks into a tuner's nook. Vela strikes a brass fork and the listeners' lamps drift
past — the room's gone deaf to them. Vela recognizes the coin's hum on sight, tells Kai it carries
the order that drowned three tiers, and refuses, flatly, to say how she'd know that.

- Dialogue (Vela): "Listen first. Then decide. You brought listeners to my door, girl."

**Panel 2** — Vela strikes a fork; the panel's hum-lines flatten to nothing
- Visual: outside, lamp-light slides past the doorway. *(PWR-003 tuning — cost: it tires her)*

**Panel 3** — Vela takes the coin, reads it for a half-second, sets it down like it's hot
- Dialogue (Vela): "That's the Sealing order. Somebody kept the proof."

**Panel 4** — Vela's burn-scarred hand closes over the torn place on her coat where a crest used to be
- Dialogue (Vela): "I say a lot of things. Sleep here. Don't touch my forks."
