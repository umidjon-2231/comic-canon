# S01-005 — The Warden's Name

## Beat
Kai presses the coin to the silent door and commands it to remember how to open — a command far
past her tier. Vela rings the fork to bleed off the cost, and still it nearly takes Kai under.
Inside: shelves of drowned records, and on every one, the same recorded voice. The Warden has a
name now. Sael.
